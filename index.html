VT Fish & Wildlife Dept.

Fish stocking schedule for 2013

Vermont has a wealth of great fishing opportunities to enjoy. If you enjoy caching Trout in a cold mountain stream or pond, we have it. If you would rather pull a spinner through lillypads chasing giant pike, we have it. If trolling the deep water for Lake Trout is more your speed, we have it. Or if you would rather fish topwater at dusk for big bass, we have it.

So get out there and take advantage of all that we have. Be safe and remember if you have the opportunity, take a kid fishing.

Learn more at the Vermont Fish & Wildlife Department website.
